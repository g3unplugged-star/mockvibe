Q1 Correct

Question: If the selling price of an article is doubled, then its profit triples. Find the profit percent.

Your Answer: A (100%) ✓

Solution: Let CP = x, SP = y. Profit = y - x. When SP doubles to 2y, profit becomes 2y - x = 3(y - x). Solving gives y = 2x, so profit = x, profit% = (x/x)*100 = 100%.
